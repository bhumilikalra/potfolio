Buddy

UX Design

Buddy is phone gap application for international study or work permit holders who need help related to situations they may face on their arrival in Vancouver. This can range from helping finding a room to applying MSP. This application works as a volunteer platform listing volunteers available.

Persona

The user research resulted in having the focus on two primary users to target and to understand their needs and frustrations points, I worked on creating the persona featuring from two different area of an international immigrant landing in Vancouver.

Wireframes

The application has two interfaces, seeker for the international person who is new to Vancouver and the volunteer who is living in Vancouver and efficient enough to provide help to new comers with all possible aspects.
