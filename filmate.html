Filmate

UI/UX Design

Filmate is a multi-platform mobile application, that helps a couple or group of six people to agree on a movie to watch. It provides a fun, non-compromising way of picking a movie.

Problem Statement :

The weekend nights are meant to shed off the tiredness and boredom of the entire week with friends or partners. The pandemic has made people stay home and relax themselves by watching movies. But agreeing to a movie to watch together can sometimes be a compromising task. People waste a lot of time choosing and agreeing on a movie without reaching a consensus.

My role in this project was to design the user screens for authentication and profile. Interaction was enhanced by adding some graphics to the mood filter cards using the idea of making the alphabets convey the meaning.

Wireframes

Following the research results, we harmonized the outcomes and created the low fidelity screens. These became a foundation to build the user interactional and high fidelity designs.
